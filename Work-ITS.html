Student Coordinator - University of Michigan ITS

At the University of Michigan Information Technology Services in Ann Arbor, Michigan, I have held the position of Student Neighborhood IT Analyst since August 2022, delivering comprehensive IT support across multiple operating systems to a workforce of over 2000 employees. In May 2023, I advanced to the role of Student Coordinator, where I manage and oversee student teams to ensure the successful execution of large-scale device updates and thorough stockroom audits, consistently meeting project deadlines while maintaining high standards of quality. I led the development and implementation of an extensive training program for student employees, producing over 50 pages of detailed documentation. Additionally, I have exhibited leadership and mentorship by training and supervising a team of 8 student employees while helping foster a collaborative and productive work environment.
